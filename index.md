Privacy Policy – Fortune Cookie Duel

Last updated: June 2026 

Developer: Lucky Bite Studio

1. Overview
Fortune Cookie Duel ("the App") is a mobile application available on Google Play. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.
Summary: The App does not collect, store, or share any personal data.
2. Information We Collect
The App does NOT collect:
Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
Location data
Device identifiers
Usage analytics
Crash reports sent to external servers
Any data sent to the developer or third parties
The App stores locally on your device only:
Your cookie break count (Good / Evil)
Your daily streak counter
Your daily challenge status
These are stored in a local file (statistik.json) on your device and never leave your device.
3. Internet Permission
The App requests INTERNET permission only to open the Ko-fi support page (https://ko-fi.com/fortunecookieduel) in your browser when you tap "Support Now." This action is entirely user-initiated. No data is transmitted by the App itself.
4. Third-Party Services
The App does not use:
Google Analytics
Firebase
AdMob or any advertising SDKs
Facebook SDK
Any crash reporting services
Any tracking or profiling services
The Ko-fi link opens in your device's browser. Ko-fi's own privacy policy applies if you choose to visit that page: https://ko-fi.com/privacy
5. Children's Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App contains no advertising and no in-app purchases.
6. Data Security
All data (game statistics) is stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to any server.

This relates only to viewing this web page and not to the use of the App itself.
